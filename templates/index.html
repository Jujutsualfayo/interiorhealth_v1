About the Interior Health Project

This project was a brain child of dedicated programmers who saw the need to solve a key aspect of society, one which required immediate intervention. This is also a Portfolio Project for Holberton School, showcasing our ability to create full-stack web applications.

The project began in August 2024 and was completed by October 2024. Throughout this time, we encountered numerous challenges, but the process helped us grow as developers and problem solvers.
